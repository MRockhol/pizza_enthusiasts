What's popular in pizza toppings?

There are many types of pizza, from the toppings to styles of pizza. So what are some of the types that are popular among Americans? According to The Huffington Post, their top ten pizza toppings are

  1. pepperoni
  2. mushrooms
  3. onions
  4. sausage
  5. bacon
  6. extra cheese
  7. black olives
  8. green peppers
  9. pineapples
  10. spinach

Deep Dish or Thin Crust?

Many people are split between the style of pizza crusts. Some will argue that Chicago style deep dish is better than the New York style thin crust. So who's right? We here at Pizza Enthusiasts believe that it is all about personal preference, but Billy thinks thin crust is better.
