Pizza News

Occasionally there'll be some interesting news articles about pizza. So on this page, we'd like to include a few articles that we think are interesting and maybe worthwhile for you to read. So check them out below, let us know if there are any interesting articles you've stumbled across.

Pizza and Body Language?

According to this interesting article by Vice, how you eat your pizza tells a lot about you. Strange right? Well a bunch of body language experts seem to think that it can "identify your essential personality qualities". Interesting right? Check out the article above and tell us your opinions on it.

Highway Pizza Havoc

In an article by Daily News, a truck carrying thousands of frozen pizzas, crashed and spilled loads of the pies on the highway, causing the Arkansas highway to shut down. That's insane and even more impressive that no one came to steal those frozen pizzas laid all over the highway. Or maybe they did, who knows! Check out the article above to read it and let us know what you think.
